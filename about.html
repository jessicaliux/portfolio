My Story



Hey, I'm Jess!

Growing up, I always had a fascination with art, whether that was taking drawing lessons as a kid, teaching myself how to use a camera, dancing through elementary school to college, or even making digital art. In any event, I was inspired by my surroundings to continuously learn and create.

There is something special about creating through different means. Each of them serve the purpose to make someone feel a certain way when they see or experience it. That's why Interactive Design is so important. Creating experiences and interfaces that are meaningful and useful is why I want to be a UI/UX designer. I hope to bring new perspectives to the table with my unique background and skill set.

I’m also passionate about fashion, POC empowerment, pop culture, food, and innovations in the tech industry. I spend most of my free time thrifting or exploring new restaurants/places to visit. In addition, I’m interested to learn more about the future of IoT and Artificial Intelligence.
